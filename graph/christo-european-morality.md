# Christo-European morality

## Summary

Christo-European morality is identified as concealing the universal danger of mankind's deterioration. Those who recognize the extraordinary fortuitousness that has governed humanity's future, and who perceive the fate hidden under the blind confidence of modern ideas combined with this morality, suffer an anguish incomparable to any other. This morality participates in what threatens to degenerate mankind toward a gregarious, mediocre type rather than allowing for the cultivation of higher human possibilities.
