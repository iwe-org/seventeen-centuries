# Gangasrotogati

## Summary

Gangasrotogati is a Sanskrit term meaning "as the Ganges flows" that Nietzsche invokes to describe his mode of thinking and living. He uses this concept to explain his deliberate difficulty, suggesting that being understood requires refinement of interpretation and that easy-going friends who expect simplicity must be given room for misunderstanding---or dismissed entirely.
