# Epicureanism

## Summary

Epicureanism is presented as a philosophy of the weak, a "soothing medicine" sought by those who wish to end the internal war of conflicting instincts within themselves. It represents a longing for repose, undisturbedness, and final unity—the happiness of those who cannot master their contradictory nature but instead seek escape from inner conflict.
