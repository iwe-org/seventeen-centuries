# Love for mankind

## Summary

Nietzsche argues that love for mankind without a higher justification---such as loving humanity for God's sake---is mere folly and brutishness. This sentiment requires refinement and proportion from a "higher inclination" to become noble. The philosopher who first recognized that unconditional love for humanity needs a redeeming purpose is praised as one who has "flown highest" in moral understanding.
