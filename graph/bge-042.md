# 42

A new order of [Philosophers](philosophers.md) is appearing; I shall venture to baptize them by a name not without danger. As far as I understand them, as far as they allow themselves to be understood---for it is their nature to *wish* to remain something of a puzzle---these philosophers of the future might rightly, perhaps also wrongly, claim to be designated as "[Tempters](tempters.md)." This name itself is after all only an attempt, or, if it be preferred, a temptation.
