# Psychology

## Summary

Psychology, as presented in these texts, has historically been hindered by moral prejudices that prevented it from exploring the depths of human nature. The new psychology must abandon the old "soul-atomism" that treats the soul as indivisible, instead embracing conceptions like "soul as social structure of the instincts and passions." Reconceived as the morphology and development-doctrine of the Will to Power, psychology should become the queen of the sciences and the path to fundamental problems.
