# Vedanta philosophy

## Summary

Nietzsche references Vedanta philosophy in connection with Kant's epistemological project, noting that the thought of the apparent existence of the subject and the soul once held immense power on earth through Vedanta teachings. This appears in his broader critique of how modern philosophy, since Descartes, has attempted to undermine the traditional conception of the soul as a grammatical subject.
