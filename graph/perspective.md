# Perspective

## Summary

Perspective refers to the subjective, embodied way humans perceive and interpret reality. Nietzsche criticizes thinkers who dismiss perspective as mere appearance while paradoxically distrusting their own bodily senses, suggesting they secretly yearn for older metaphysical certainties like the immortal soul or God. Rather than honestly embracing perspectival knowledge, these philosophers seek to escape modern ideas by retreating to supposedly more secure foundations.
