# 53

Why [A[Theism](theism.md)](atheism.md) nowadays? "The father" in [God](god.md) is thoroughly refuted; equally so "the judge," "the rewarder." Also his "[Free Will](free-will.md)": he does not hear---and even if he did, he would not know how to help. The worst is that he seems incapable of communicating himself clearly; is he uncertain?---This is what I have made out (by questioning and listening at a variety of conversations) to be the cause of the decline of European theism; it appears to me that though the [Religious instinct](religious-instinct.md) is in vigorous growth---it rejects the theistic satisfaction with profound distrust.
