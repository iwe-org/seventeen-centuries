# Taste for the unconditional

## Summary

The taste for the unconditional is described as the worst of all tastes, characteristic of youth's inability to appreciate nuance. This tendency leads to judgments of absolute Yea and Nay toward men and things, resulting in falsification and deception that must later be painfully unlearned as one matures into the art of introducing subtlety into one's sentiments.
