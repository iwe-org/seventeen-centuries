# Justice

## Summary

Justice is the virtue governing our internal actions and social conduct, directing us to act in accordance with our rational nature for the benefit of the community. It is inseparable from right reason and the rational soul itself, demanding that we speak truth freely and act according to the worth of each situation. Marcus Aurelius presents justice not as an external obligation but as the natural expression of a soul aligned with universal law and piety.
