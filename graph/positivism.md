# Positivism

## Summary

Positivism is criticized as a patchwork philosophy that throws a "bric-a-brac of ideas of the most varied origin" onto the intellectual marketplace. It represents a "village-fair motleyness" that contains nothing genuinely new or true, provoking disgust in those of more refined taste who seek authentic thought rather than this hodgepodge of reality-philosophies.
