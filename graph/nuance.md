# Nuance

## Summary

Nuance is described as "the best gain of life"—an art that youth lacks and must painfully learn. In contrast to the youthful taste for the unconditional, which judges with crude "Yea and Nay," nuance represents the mature capacity to introduce subtlety and artfulness into one's sentiments and judgments.
