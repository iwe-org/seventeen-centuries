# Nothingness

## Summary

Nothingness represents the final stage in the evolution of religious cruelty. After humanity sacrificed human beings to God, then sacrificed their own nature and instincts, the ultimate act of cruelty became sacrificing God himself and worshipping nothingness in his place. This paradoxical mystery of worshipping stone, stupidity, gravity, fate, and nothingness is described as reserved for the rising generation.
