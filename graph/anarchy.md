# Anarchy

## Summary

Anarchy represents the threat of instinctual disorder, a condition where the hierarchy of drives within an individual or society breaks down. It is associated with corruption, signaling that the foundational emotions called "life" are being convulsed. In this view, anarchy is not merely political chaos but a deeper dissolution of the ordering principles that sustain healthy functioning.
