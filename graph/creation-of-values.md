# Creation of values

## Summary

The creation of values is the defining task of the real philosopher, distinguishing them from philosophical workers who merely formalize existing valuations. While scientific thinkers fix and systematize what has already been esteemed as "truth," genuine philosophers are commanders and lawgivers who determine the direction and purpose of mankind. Their knowing is creating, their creating is law-giving, and their will to truth is Will to Power.
