# Voltaire

## Summary

Voltaire represents the Enlightenment ideal of seeking truth for humanitarian purposes. Nietzsche critiques this approach, suggesting that those who search for truth "too humanely" will find nothing. Compared to the Abbe Galiani, Voltaire is described as less profound and consequently less silent, implying a certain superficiality in his thought.
