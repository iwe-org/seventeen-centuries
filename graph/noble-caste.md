# Noble caste

## Summary

The noble caste, in its historical origins, was always the barbarian caste. These were men of prey with unbroken strength of will and desire for power who conquered weaker, more peaceful civilizations. Their superiority lay not primarily in physical strength but in psychical power - they were "more complete men," which also implies "more complete beasts."
