# 49

That which is so astonishing in the religious life of the [Ancient Greeks](ancient-greeks.md) is the irrestrainable stream of *[Gratitude](gratitude.md)* which it pours forth---it is a very superior kind of man who takes *such* an attitude towards nature and life.---Later on, when the populace got the upper hand in Greece, *[Fear](fear.md)* became rampant also in religion; and [Christianity](christianity.md) was preparing itself.
