# Plebeianism

## Summary

Plebeianism refers to a cultural atmosphere that renders everything "opaque and leaden," making it difficult to recognize true nobility. Nietzsche describes it as a "heavy overcast sky" under which the noble man must be identified not by actions or works, but by the fundamental certainty and self-reverence of the noble soul.
