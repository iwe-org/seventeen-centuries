# Beyond Good and Evil

## Summary

"Beyond Good and Evil" represents a philosophical stance that transcends traditional moral valuations of truth and falsehood. It is the position of recognizing untruth as a condition of life, acknowledging that logical fictions and false opinions are indispensable for human existence. This formula distinguishes genuine free spirits from mere "freethinkers" who advocate modern democratic ideals, marking instead those who understand that severity, danger, and the Will to Power serve human elevation as much as their opposites.
