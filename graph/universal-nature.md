# Universal Nature

## Summary

Universal Nature is the governing principle of the cosmos that orchestrates all change and assigns each part its proper role. Marcus Aurelius teaches that everything useful to the whole is good and timely, and that the universe cannot generate anything harmful to itself. By recognizing ourselves as parts of this greater whole, we should accept what is assigned to us and resign ourselves to universal nature's workings, finding contentment in alignment with its design.
