# Opinion

## Summary

Opinion is the mental judgment we attach to events and circumstances, and it lies entirely within our power to control. Marcus Aurelius teaches that most disturbances come not from things themselves but from our opinions about them. By choosing to cast away harmful opinions, we can find inner calm and make any hardship endurable.
