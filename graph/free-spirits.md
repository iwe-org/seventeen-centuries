# Free Spirits

## Summary

Free spirits are distinguished from mere "freethinkers" who advocate democratic equality and modern ideas. True free spirits embrace solitude, personal independence, and intellectual adventure, recognizing that human greatness emerges through hardship rather than comfort. They serve as heralds and forerunners of the philosophers of the future, maintaining rigorous honesty as their defining virtue while working toward a transvaluation of values.
