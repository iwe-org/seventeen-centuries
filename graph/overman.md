# Overman

## Summary

The Overman represents a higher mode of existence characterized by the capacity for "golden laughter." In Nietzsche's view, even the gods philosophize and laugh in an Overman-like fashion, finding ridicule and joy even in holy matters. This suggests the Overman transcends conventional seriousness and achieves a divine lightness of spirit.
