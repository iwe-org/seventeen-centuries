# Puritanism

## Summary

Puritanism is cited alongside Stoicism and Port Royal as an example of the constraint and discipline under which freedom, elegance, and mastery develop. Such moral systems function as a kind of tyranny against nature and reason, yet this very long obedience in the same direction proves to be the disciplinary means whereby strength and spiritual refinement are attained.
