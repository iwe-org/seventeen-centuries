# Human soul

## Summary

The human soul represents the vast domain of inner human experience, encompassing its heights, depths, and distances throughout history. Nietzsche presents it as the "hunting-domain" for the psychologist, a virgin forest of possibilities that remains largely unexhausted. Understanding its full range requires profound personal experience, particularly when investigating problems like knowledge and conscience in religious souls.
