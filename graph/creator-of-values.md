# Creator of values

## Summary

In Nietzsche's analysis of master-morality, the noble type of man is characterized as a creator of values who does not require external approval or validation. He determines what is good based on what he recognizes in himself, conferring honour on things through his own judgment rather than adhering to pre-existing moral standards. This self-legislation of values is inseparable from self-glorification and the consciousness of overflowing power.
