# Slave-insurrection in morals

## Summary

The slave-insurrection in morals refers to a radical inversion of values that began with the Jewish people, whose prophets fused "rich," "godless," "wicked," "violent," and "sensual" into a single reproachful category while elevating "poor" to mean "saint" and "friend." This transvaluation gave life on earth a new and dangerous charm for millennia, turning the word "world" itself into a term of condemnation.
