# Nobility/noble

## Summary

The noble is not established by actions, which are always ambiguous, nor by works, but by a fundamental certainty that the noble soul possesses about itself. This certainty is something not to be sought, found, or lost; it is simply present. The noble soul has reverence for itself, and this inner belief determines the order of rank.
