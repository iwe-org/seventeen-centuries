# Reverence

## Summary

Reverence is presented as a fundamental quality of the noble soul—a self-directed respect that is not earned through actions or works but exists as an inherent certainty. Unlike those who merely long for nobility, the truly noble possess this reverence naturally; it cannot be sought, found, or lost. This self-reverence distinguishes authentic nobility from the mere aspiration to it.
